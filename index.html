Who we are

ACE Diversity and Inclusion Experts (ACE-Di) is a consulting firm resourced with professionals with global experience in disability and diversity inclusion in Kenya and Africa at large.

  • ACE-Di works with non-profit, civil and corporate entities to build their capacity on disability-inclusive strategem
  • We conduct accessibility audit assessments to enhance inclusive access to the built and social environment.
  • we deliver high-level and implementable recommendations and guidelines for sustainable inclusion at the workplace

ACE-DI has worked with various nonprofit, civil, and corporate entities to create more inclusive and accessible spaces and opportunities for organizations and institutions to become more inclusive by building their capacity on disability-inclusive strategies, diversity models as disability, and diversity-inclusive etiquette.

One billion people, or 15% of the worldâ€™s population, experience some form of disability.

Persons with disabilities are more likely to experience adverse socioeconomic outcomes such as less education, poorer health outcomes, lower levels of employment, and higher poverty rates.

Barriers to full social and economic inclusion of persons with disabilities include:

  • Inaccessible physical environments and transportation.
  • The unavailability of assistive devices and technologies.
  • Non-adapted means of communication and gaps in service delivery
  • Discriminatory prejudice and stigma in society.

Workplace accessibility assessments

Such assessments include and are performed as per the following:

  • Conducting rapid accessibility assessments to ensure inclusive access during public events, conferences, seminars, workshops, AGMs, church events, community meetings, and general elections among others
  • Conducting rapid assessments for an inclusive workplace for all employees
  • Providing sign language interpretation services during public events and inclusive mass mobilization/awareness targeting persons with disability.
  • Identifying & interviewing potential employees with a disability based on company operations.
